The challenge

The role changed. The playbook didn't.

The world does not slow down for leaders. Most were promoted for being excellent at the work — then handed a role that asks for something else entirely.

01

Relentless pace

Rapid change and pressure leave no room to lead deliberately.

02

Execution drift

Small misalignments and unclear expectations quietly erode results.

03

Hard conversations

Avoided or "winged" — the moments that matter most go unhandled.

04

Isolation at the top

Leading alone, with no place to think clearly or be challenged well.

"Poor leadership doesn't show up on a P&L. It shows up at your employees' dinner table." Leadership today isn't about having the answers — it's about creating clarity, trust, and momentum through others.

Your invitation

Here's to answering the call.

The Fall 2026 cohort is opening a limited number of seats. Start with the 4-minute assessment — and see exactly how TLC meets you where you are.

What is the time commitment?+

Weekly live virtual sessions on Thursdays, 9–11 AM PST, across six months — plus a 10-week independent intersession and two private 1:1 coaching sessions.

Who is this for?+

Advancing leaders on the path to the C-Suite who want to lead through people — not around them. Cohorts can also be run privately for a single company's team.

What's included?+

All weekly training and materials, a shipped physical workbook plus digital versions, two 1:1 coaching sessions, and lifetime access to the resource library after you complete.

What happens after I start the assessment?+

You'll see your personalized leadership snapshot and how TLC maps to it, then a short path to reserve your seat in the cohort and complete enrollment.
